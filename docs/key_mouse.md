# Keyboard and Mouse Manual

## Keyboard Manual

### Move Ball
Use the `W`, `A`, `S`, and `D` keys to move the ball.

### Place Robot
Press the `R` key to place the selected robot. The robot is selected by clicking it with the left click.

### Place Ball Inside the Field
Press the `P` key to place the ball. The ball will be positioned 0.5 meters away from the wall.


## Mouse Manual

### Left Click
- Click on the field to adjust the camera view.
- Click on a robot to select and move it.

### Right Click
- Click on the field to place the ball at the clicked coordinates.
